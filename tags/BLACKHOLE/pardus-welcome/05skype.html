Communicate with your friends using Skype You can call anyone else on Skype, anywhere in the world for free. And you’ll always be able to do that. There are some other useful things you can do on Skype like,
  • With SkypeOut, you can use Skype to call landlines and mobiles, all over the world.
  • SkypeIn is a real phone number your friends can call. You pick up the call in Skype.
  • Skype Voicemail takes messages for you when you’re busy or offline.
