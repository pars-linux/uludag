Touch your photos with elegance Digikam is a simple digital photo management application which makes importing and organizing digital photos easy. The photos can be organized in albums which are automatically sorted chronologically. An easy-to-use interface is provided to connect to your camera, preview images, and download or delete them.

The people who inspired digiKam's design are the photographers like you who want to view, manage, edit, enhance, organize, tag, and share photographs under Linux.
