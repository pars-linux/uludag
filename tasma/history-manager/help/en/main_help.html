History Manager

General Information

History Manager utility, lets you to take your system's snapshot using Pardus package manager infrastructure, and allows turn backs which provide backup's of packages and configuration files on a system.

With history manager, as well as turning to a snapshot point, you can take back your system to a specific package manager operation. Advantage of snapshot points is an additional, configuration files backup support.

Taking a snapshot

This operation will take a snapshot of your system. Just click on "New Snapshot" button in "History" tab, and operation will start. This can take up to 1-2 minutes depending on your system power.

Turning back to a point

With this operation, you can turn back to a snapshot or other operation point. Select the date (operation) to turn back to, and click "Restore" from within the "History" tab.
