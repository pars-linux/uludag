Pardus 1.1 on the way!
Pardus Linux is on its way with many recent Linux technologies and solutions in its new version, including state-of-the-art features such as AppArmor, Xen virtualization machine support and authentic technologies such as Mudur (a Python driven fast and effective init system), COMAR (the configuration manager) and improved PiSi (package manager).
