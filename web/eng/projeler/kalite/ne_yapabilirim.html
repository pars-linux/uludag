:: What can I do? ::

First of all you can check the ongoing projects from this web pages, contact the responsible member of a project you are interested in through the Quality list or you may take the responsiblity of one of the waiting projects of your interest.

Moreover, you may send an e-mail to the e-mail list of the quality team introducing yourself and including which of the following you can contribute to by being a member of the quality team so that we may ask for your help on the whole or part of one or more issues listed below whenever needed:

   - Documentation: You can proof-read our documents and report errors, join the documentation work, internationalize our documents, write guides for applications, and volunteer to prepare various presentation and advertisement materials.
   - Web Pages: You may take care of updating the Web pages, internationalize them, update the news, prepare and announce the bulletins.
   - Software Development: You can tell us about the programming languages you have expertise on and enjoy yourself developing software, and describe the projects you have developed or contributed to before, if any.
   - Test: You may test the developed applications, the usability of the user-interfaces, the beta versions of the distribution and root folder systems. This way, we can ask for your help on issues that need espescially be considered, intensively tested, and tried with different hardware configurations.
   - Packages: You can apply for packages of applications which you have been using for a long time and have expertise on part of the distribution or those you want to be included in the distribution. You may demand to take over all of the responsiblity of this package.
   - Presentation: You can arrange seminars to advertise the Uludag Project and Pardus. This way, we can, for example, direct the seminar requests of the universities in your town to you, and ask you to represent us as an Uludag Developer in activities.

In addition, you can volunteer to some job not mentioned here, start a new project not taking part among the waiting projects.

Please read the quality document before joining the list and do not neglect to introduce yourself as stated there.
