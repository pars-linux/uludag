The first product of Uludag has been released:
Pardus National Operating System Live CD

Pardus Live CD, the bootable CD version of the Pardus National Operating System, has been announced and distributed to the participants of Akademik Bilisim in Gaziantep University. Live CD includes office tools (word processor, spreadsheet editor, presentation tool, etc.), Internet tools (browser, e-mail, instant messaging, relay chat etc. clients), multimedia and graphics tools (video player, music player, etc.), games and lots of other applications.

Pardus is opensource and freely distributed as a GPL (General Public License) product. This decision had been announced in the Uludag Weekly Bulletin on 30 Jan 2005.

You can freely download Pardus Live CD iso image and md5sum value.

(Thanks to Linux Users Group for hosting Pardus Live CD iso image by reason of traffic density on our server)
