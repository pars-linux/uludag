Pardus 2008 Release Delayed

After the release of Pardus 2008 RC2 have been delayed for two days, it was discussed to delay the final release from Monday to Friday as well, for having a wider test time for the new features and the packages in the repositories.
More information can be obtained here.

Pardus 2008 will be available on June 27, Friday with Turkish spellchecking available right on the desktop, a fast and simple installation procedure, ease of use, and a set of Internet, multimedia, development and editing applications suitable for desktop system users' needs as always.

Pardus products are available for free use and distribution under the terms of the GNU GPL.

Updated calendar of pre-release versions is as follows:

Tuesday, 3 June : Pardus 2008 Beta
Monday, 9 June : Pardus 2008 RC1
Wednesday, 18 June : Pardus 2008 RC2
Friday, 27 June : Pardus 2008
