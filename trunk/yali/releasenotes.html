Pardus 1.0 Release Notes

 Welcome to Pardus, an easy to install and use system, with many enhancements to desktop usability, compatibility and security. With Pardus, you can connect to Internet, read your e-mails, securily communicate with your friends and exploit your computer's multimedia capability by listening to music and watching movies. We kindly invite you to http://www.uludag.org.tr/eng to explore new Pardus opportunities.

Basic system requirements

While Pardus will work on any modest hardware, we recommend that you have at least 256 Mb of RAM and 1800 Mhz CPU. While these are two very basic conditions to let Pardus work, the minimal requirements can be set as follows:
  • 196 Mb of RAM (at least 256 Mb recommended)
  • 1 GHz Intel or AMD CPU (at least 1800 MHz recommended)
We also recoommend you to use an ethernet or wireless card to connect to internet other Pardus services, in order to download more productivity software. A sound card will automatically be detected and used after you install Pardus.
