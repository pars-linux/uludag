How can you help?



Pardus is different from other distributions: it's a community driven Linux distribution developed by open minded people. We find real solutions to real problems. Our own package manager, hardware configurator, Python based init system, configuration manager etc all make Pardus a unique distribution.

Pardus developers believe in the community involvement, so all the discussions take place transparently. Now Pardus project is looking for volunteers to foster its innovative work. Whether you are a developer, translator, quality manager or bugfixer, we have an open seat in our plane. Join Pardus team today.

Join our mailing lists

Pardus mailing lists are hosted on mailman. The first mailing list of your concern can be pardus-devel or pardus-users. Download and install Pardus, then join our team.

Join us on IRC

IRC chat provides a convenient way to solve our problems, if any. Just point your IRC client to a server and join #pardus channel. All the time you'll find helpful people lurking on the channel :)

Translate Pardus in your language

Be the first to translate Pardus to speak in your language! If you are brave enough, just click on the translation project and see what Pardus can do for you. Pardus 1.1 currently speaks Dutch and Turkish, and moreover Italian and French is underway.

Make some packages for Pardus

PİSİ is a package manager, designed to be easy and straightforward. With PİSİ and its XML package format, you can build packages in a few minutes. In order to have an understanding of how PİSİ works, click here. There's also a small howto for the beginners.
