FAQ

Is it Uludağ or Uludag?

The name of the project is Uludağ, which approximately pronounced as ou-loo-duh. To prevent any UTF-8 related problems in case our non-Turkish speaking visitors have not loaded the appropriate fonts, we use the ISO-8859-1 friendly Uludag throughout.

What does Uludag mean, anyway?

Uludag is a quasi-acronym formed by the first three letters of words Ulusal Dagitim, which is National Distribution in Turkish. Uludag, is also the name of a mountain 150 km to south-east of Istanbul and 350 km to west of Ankara, which is infamous for its ski resorts. The highest peak of Uludag is 2543 m (8344 ft) from sea level. Uludag has its place in the Greek mythology as Mount Olympos, in fact Olympos means Uludag, that is empyreal mountain.

Will the distribution be named Uludag?

No. Uludag is the name for the project for national distribution development. Nevertheless, the distribution is also being referred as Uludag throughout this site, since it has not been named properly, yet.

Who supports Uludag? How will its continuity be guaranteed?

Uludag project is being carried out under the auspices of TUBITAK UEKAE. The distribution will be developed and released in the scope of this project. Among the larger goals of the project is establishing a sustainable organization to develop this distribution. This organization will be able to found its own resources through creative business models that will be developed and thus will not require external funding.

Is Uludag different than Linux?

Linux, is the kernel of the operating system. A Linux distribution gathers the kernel, tools and application software from varied sources, provides interoperability to this collection and exposes a common management framework. The distribution relieves an ordinary user from acquiring the kernel and all the tools individually and to spend time and effort to make them operate together flawlessly. Uludag is a Linux distribution, thus uses the Linux kernel and several related tools. The value add is the easy installation and use of the resulting operating system.

Is Uludag going to be sold?

The general purpose version of Uludag will be released under GPL licensing rules, and be available free of charge. GPL will be the first choice for licensing of Uludag projects and software..

What does open source mean? What is GPL?

When you acquire open source software you get the source code as well. You are free to see how the program works, change the code if you wish to, use and distribute the modified version. You will be sure of your security, in charge of your data, and controlling your performance if you use open source software. GPL (General Public License) is the license agreement that guarantees that you are free to execute all these. GPL is prepared and distributed by the Free Software Foundation (www.fsf.org) and makes you the real owner of the software you use.
